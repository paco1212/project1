Roommate Experience Mobile Application
Class Project (ENTR390: Digital Product Design)

There are lots of incoming college students who have never had the experience to live in a shared room with strangers or friends. As a result, it is generally a good idea for college roommates to make agreements together that will outline their chores, rules, supplies so that the students could have a positive living experience. However, college roommates still need to be able to manage these agreements somehow and old each other accountable to them. Unfortunately, not everyone has a huge white board like my friend to track these shared-agreements with their roommates. Immediately, I recognized that there was a need - college roommates want something that will track their house/room chores and roommate responsibilities so that they can stay organized and establish ground rules of their living space.
